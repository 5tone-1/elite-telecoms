Business Broadband

Unlike other service providers, we only provide broadband to business customers. We deliver high quality networks that are reliable, secure and resilient to high demand. This means that you will always receive the best data speeds that your line can achieve without being constrained by the limitations of the service provider's network. Elite Telecoms can provide a range of business grade solutions that help you make the most of your Fibre Broadband line including Hosted VoIP, SIP Trunks, VPN and data storage.

SOGEA

SOGEA (Single Order Generic Ethernet Access) is the latest FTTC broadband which is a broadband only service that doesn't require an analogue voice line to work. This improves the reliability of the broadband as well reducing costs.

FTTP Broadband

FTTP (Fibre To The Premises) is the ultrafast broadband technology with speeds of up to 330Mb using a technology capable of gigabit speeds and holds fantastic promise for the future. FTTP uses fibre all of the way from the exchange to your premises and is a step up from SOGEA broadband.

Fibre Leased Line

When upload is just as important as download a leased line provides a symmetrical connection. This means upload is just as fast as download. Different to SOGEA & FTTP, a leased line does not use the Public network and is dedicated for your use so speed is never compromised. Speeds up to 1,000Mbps (1 gigabit)
