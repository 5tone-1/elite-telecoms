Hosted PBX

Cloud-based business phone systems have the ability to bring stunning new functionality into your business whilst lowering costs and maintenance. What is cloud telephony exactly? Instead of storing the network and routing elements of a business phone system within your office on site, cloud-based telephony takes everything offsite. This system is hosted in the cloud, which means your data is securely stored on a server that can be accessed via the internet. Regardless of how big or small your company may be, cloud-based telephony will work for businesses of any size. Designed to improve the efficiency of your business, there are plenty of advantages to switching to a cloud telephony system.

PBX Systems

PBX stands for Private Branch Exchange System, which is a private telephone network used on site within a company or organization. The users of the PBX phone system can communicate within the company and with the outside world, using different communication channels like Voice over IP or SIP. Additionally, it provides features that everyone these days use like voicemail, call recording, interactive voice menus (IVRs), call queues, Music on Hold, transferring and holding calls. Connectivity

Communication with the outside world is changing. Analogue & ISDN lines, as technologies, are to be phased out and will be a thing of the past from 2025 onwards. VOIP or SIP will be the replacement technology. SIP trunking is already here and is becoming the main way to carry voice traffic over IP networks using data connectivity which in turn offers a lot more flexibility at a fraction of the cost. We at Elite Telecoms (Dorset) Ltd will be happy to discuss what this means for your business.
